Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, cilantro, parsley, and basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Add the salad ingredients to the bowl and toss to combine. Serve immediately.
  3. Enjoy your salad!
